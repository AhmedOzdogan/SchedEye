SchedEye

Made by a teacher. Shared with the world.

Why I Built It

I’m an English teacher who got tired of juggling schedules, payments, and student info across messy spreadsheets and random notes. I built SchedEye to solve my own frustration — and realized it could help others too.

SchedEye is a free, simple, and teacher-friendly solution for managing your classes with peace of mind.

What Can You Do With SchedEye?

Plan Classes

Organize your weekly and monthly schedule clearly and easily.

Track Payments

See who paid, who didn’t, and stay in control financially.

Get Reminders

Receive alerts for unpaid sessions or upcoming lessons.

Stay Organized

A clean, modern interface made just for teachers.

SchedEye is completely free to use.

No ads. No subscriptions. Just something useful for teachers.
