How to Use SchedEye

Explore features, watch quick GIF demos, and learn how to get the most out of SchedEye.

Update User Details

You can easily update your username, password, and currency preferences. Just click your username at the top right and choose Settings.

Adding a New Class

To add a new class, click the Add Lesson button at the top, fill in the class name, date, time, and school information, then click Save.

Edit Lesson

You can edit your lessons without deleting them. Simply right-click on a lesson, select Edit, and update the details as needed.

Update Payments

Easily update the payment status for one or multiple lessons. Right-click on a lesson and choose Paid or Unpaid. You can also select multiple lessons at once to update them together.

Deleting Lessons

Easily remove lessons from your schedule. Select the lessons you want to delete, then right-click and choose Delete. You can delete one or multiple lessons at once.

Duplicating Lessons

Easily create copies of existing lessons. To duplicate a class, select it, right-click, and choose Duplicate. Then, pick the new date and time â€” your lesson will be copied instantly.

Duplicating Weeks

Easily copy an entire week of lessons. Select all the classes from the week, then choose the week you want to copy them to from the menu. All selected lessons will be duplicated to the new week without hassle.

Lesson Details

View detailed information for each lesson from the View menu. From there, you can also edit, duplicate, or delete lessons as needed.

Calculate Total Hours

Quickly calculate the total hours for your lessons. Click Calculate at the top right to view your teaching hours by school, month, year, or overall.
